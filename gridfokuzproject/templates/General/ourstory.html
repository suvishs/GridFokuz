Celebrating Everyday

Grid Fokuz is an idea celebrating the Extraordinary of Everyday. Before the launch of Grid Fokuz in the year 2016, we have been working through various Industries in different markets for almost two decades, hence while working in Kerala market we were able to identify the gigantic gap between the client’s requirement for corporate gifts & gifting solutions and the way they select them. Therefore it unwrapped a beautiful opportunity for us to bridge that gap.

Genesis

The genesis of the name ‘Grid Fokuz’ itself originates from the idea of staying within the Grid and focusing on the actual needs of the Clients. We go through systematic and planned market research to understand the Clients’ need, bringing forth the most appropriate gifts and gifting solutions, which not only add value to their relations but also speaks a lot about their brand’s status. At Grid Fokuz our objective is the happiness of our clients and their business growth which in turn is our happiness and growth Therefore we travel all across the country in search of innovative products and aspiring manufacturers so that we can present our clients with the most innovative gifts.

What we do

We majorly focus our attention on the customization need of our clients. Because of this, we have started our own manufacturing units of Bags, T-Shirts, Umbrellas, Acrylic Products, Calendars & Diaries. We are also corporate dealers of the majority of top brands in India like apparel brands, electronic brands, household utility brands, and so on. Apart from this, we also cater to traditional gifts dealing with all varieties of corporate gifts with more than 1200 products in our product range. Joy and amusement on the face of our client is the world that we aspire to every day so that is why we say at Grid Fokuz The Fokuz is on You.
